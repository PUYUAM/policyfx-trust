## 📜 Your #1 Origin Story: *Míng Chè*
> *“Míng Chè” is the name of still water under morning light — clear, deep, and quietly revealing. It reflects how you approach problems: not with force, but with patient observation and precise insight. The ‘sun’ radical grounds it in warmth and clarity; the ‘water’ radical adds flow and adaptability. It’s a name elders will respect, colleagues will remember, and you’ll feel proud to say — whether ordering coffee in Shanghai or introducing yourself at a Berlin tech meetup. Pronounced Míng (like “meeng”) Chè (like “chuh” with a falling tone).*

- *Chè*: Sharp, falling tone — ends like “chuh” with a quick drop.
